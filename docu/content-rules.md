# Content Rules

## Purpose

This file defines the rules for all written content on the website.

The goal is to ensure that all text on the website is:

- professional
- clear
- trustworthy
- concise
- suitable for a premium chauffeur / taxi service in Switzerland

---

## Language Requirement

The final website must be **entirely written in German**.

This includes:

- headlines
- section titles
- body text
- navigation
- buttons
- labels
- CTAs
- small UI text

No English should appear in the visible UI.

German should be **natural and professional**, not machine-translated sounding.

---

## Tone of Voice

The tone should feel:

- professionell
- seriös
- modern
- vertrauenswürdig
- serviceorientiert

It should match a **premium chauffeur / airport transfer service**.

Avoid:

- overly aggressive marketing language
- exaggerated luxury claims
- slang
- casual language
- emojis
- hype language

The tone should feel calm and confident.

---

## Writing Style

Content should be:

- concise
- easy to scan
- structured
- professional

Prefer:

- short paragraphs
- clear headings
- bullet points where useful
- benefit-oriented sentences

Avoid long text blocks.

---

## Headline Style

Headlines should be:

- clear
- confident
- professional
- concise

Example style:

Good:

"Premium Taxi & Chauffeur Service in Zürich"

"Komfortable Flughafentransfers"

"Zuverlässiger Chauffeurservice"

Avoid:

- overly creative headlines
- vague marketing phrases
- long complicated sentences

---

## CTA Language

Calls to action should be short and direct.

Recommended CTA texts:

- Jetzt anrufen
- Transfer anfragen
- Fahrt anfragen
- Kontakt aufnehmen
- E-Mail senden

Avoid long CTA labels.

Buttons should use **2–4 words maximum**.

---

## Service Descriptions

Service descriptions should be short and clear.

Example style:

**Flughafentransfer**

Zuverlässige und komfortable Fahrten von und zum Flughafen Zürich – pünktlich, diskret und stressfrei.

Keep service descriptions short.

Focus on benefits such as:

- Komfort
- Zuverlässigkeit
- Pünktlichkeit
- Premium Fahrzeuge
- persönlicher Service

---

## Vehicle Descriptions

Vehicle descriptions should emphasize comfort and premium service.

Example style:

**BMW 7 Series**

Luxuriöse Limousine mit höchstem Komfort – ideal für Geschäftsreisen und exklusive Fahrten.

Avoid overly technical car details.

Focus on the passenger experience.

---

## Driver Introduction

The driver introduction should feel personal but professional.

Tone:

- trustworthy
- respectful
- service oriented

Example structure:

Introduction of the driver  
Explanation of the service philosophy  
Focus on reliability and comfort

Avoid:

- long personal stories
- informal language
- excessive self-promotion

---

## Popular Routes Section

The "Beliebte Fahrten" section should present routes clearly.

Example format:

Flughafen Zürich → Luzern  
ca. 68 km

Short description optional.

Keep text minimal and functional.

---

## Trust Section

The trust section should highlight key advantages.

Use short statements such as:

- Professioneller Chauffeurservice
- Moderne Premium Fahrzeuge
- Pünktliche Flughafentransfers
- Komfort & Diskretion
- 24/7 verfügbar

Avoid long explanations.

---

## Paragraph Length

Keep paragraphs short.

Recommended:

- 1–3 sentences per paragraph

Avoid long blocks of text.

---

## Clarity First

Content should always prioritize clarity.

Visitors should immediately understand:

- what service is offered
- where the service operates
- why it is reliable
- how to contact

Never sacrifice clarity for marketing language.

---

## Local Relevance

Content should clearly reference:

- Zürich
- Flughafen Zürich
- Glattbrugg
- Schweizweite Transfers

This improves trust and relevance.

---

## SEO Considerations

The content should naturally include important keywords such as:

- Taxi Zürich
- Flughafentransfer Zürich
- Chauffeur Service Zürich
- Transfer Flughafen Zürich

However, avoid keyword stuffing.

Write naturally.

---

## Content Quality Check

Before finalizing content, verify:

1. Is the language natural German?
2. Does the tone feel professional?
3. Are sentences short and clear?
4. Is the text easy to scan?
5. Does it sound credible for a premium service?

If not, revise.

---

## Content Priority

When writing content, prioritize:

1. clarity
2. professionalism
3. readability
4. trust
5. conversion

Never prioritize marketing hype over clarity.
